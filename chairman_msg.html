Chairman's Message
Respected Members,
It gives me immense pleasure to extend a warm welcome on behalf of the Board of Directors and on my personal behalf to each one of you at this 3rd Annual General Meeting of the Dr.babasaheb Ambedkar Multistate Coop Credit Soc.Ltd.The Annual Report containing the Directors' Report and audited financial statements for the year ended 31st March, 2014 has been made available to the members and with your consent I deem it as read. Transformation in banking sector today is propelled by the changing aspirations of the customers. In tune with the changing customer preferences, the Society has been continuously innovating and ensuring customer delight through products and service enhancements.

THE YEAR 2013-14 IN REVIEW

I wish to place on record that, in the year 2013-14 Society has recorded increase in its deposits by 5.69 %, advances by 26.68 % and net profit by 25.00 %. In the above backdrop, the overall performance of the Society was satisfactory in the year 2013-14. Keeping in view the need to build a healthy Net worth to take care of future growth plans and also to reward the shareholders appropriately for the year 2013-14.

In this year we took aggressive steps for expansion of branches with a 74.1% growth in its network. We have opened 20 new branches in the year 2013-2014 and 6 new branches during the year 2014-15 across the Maharashtra and Karnataka state. The total network of branches is reached to 53 by the end of June 2014. Society has opened three ATM centers one at Osmanabad one at Arvindnagar, and one at Dhoki. A Mega banking Centre is opend in Osmanabad in front of S.T. Bus stand and further expansion of ATM Center is at Lohara and Latur branch is made in the current year. The total network of ATM centers is reached o 5 ATM by the end of June 2014.

CUSTOMER SERVICE AND TECHNOLOGY:
We have relationship with our customer that is built over the years on trust. We are committed for bringing to our esteemed customers the best of banking services, products and a tradition of excellence. Taking forward the innovation in customer service, the Society has started ATM Service for its customers along with cash deposit and passbook printing kiosks. Society has also become the channel partner of ICICI BANK'S Cash Management Service system (CMS) to offer instant interbank electronic fund transfer service (RTGS/NEFT) from all branches of society. The above facilities make service delivery prompt, efficient and effective. We are looking forward to reaching out to more number of customers with highest level of customer satisfaction.

During the financial year we have focused on to open the accounts of Soya bean Growers who desires to supply their Soya bean directly to ADM Ltd. and the accounts of farmers supplying chana, Tur and other food grains to the procurement centre of NCDEX, Venkeys, and NAFED. By doing aggressive marketing of opening of new saving accounts our team has opened around 3000 new accounts. Society has decided to implement Central Govt. Scheme of pension under National Pension Scheme through our society. For that purpose society has signed a MOU. A person between 18 to 60 years age group can participate in this scheme by depositing minimum Rs 1000 per annum. We are launching the said scheme within a month's time. We are going to launch our website; the domain name will be www.ambedkarmultistate.com Recently society has started Lockers facility in 7 branches namely, Tambri Osmanabad, Arvindnagar, Gandhi chowk latur, Washi, Khamaswadi, Umarga and Bhosari Pune.

ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS
I take this opportunity to express my sincere thanks and gratitude to all the members of the society for reposing their faith and confidence in the Management of the society. I appreciate and thank the Members of the Board, and Vice-Chairman for their valuable guidance, support and prudent counsel. I place on record my sincere thanks to the officials of Central Registrar of Co-operative Societies & other officials of Ministry of Agriculture & Co-operation, Govt. of India, Commissioners and Registrars of Co-operative Societies of and Maharashtra and Karnataka and other Statutory & Regulatory authorities for their valuable guidance and support extended from time to time. I express my gratitude to all the customers, Depositors, Members, employees and well-wishers for their loyalty, patronage and confidence which have always remained a source of inspiration and strength for the society. I also thank and acknowledge the contributions made by the Auditors, Consultants.I am once again thankful for your support in the growth of the society and hope that I will receive your warm wishes and blessings Jai Sahakar, Jai hind, Jai Maharashtra.

Thanks.

Jai Shankar, Jai Hind, Jai Maharashtra!
Your Sincerely
Arvind Gore
Chairman
