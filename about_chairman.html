Bio-data of Hon. Chairman
Name Mr.Arvind Janardhan Gore

Mr.Arvind Gore started his carrier in Co-Operative & Social sector at year 1970. He was involved in establishment of first Co-Operative sugar factory Terna Sahakari Sakhar Karkhana Ltd; of Marathvada region. His dedication, devotion & spark was noticed and he was appointed as Chairman of the factory.

During his tenure factory was expanded and flourished to become leading sugar factory of the region.

Arvind Gore having huge experience over 40 years in co-operative sector realized need of cooperative sugar factory in the area around Osmanabad district. Anticipating and visualizing upcoming irrigation facilities in the area he conceptualized establishment of a new sugar factory in order to change and uplift the life of the rural farmers of the area.

His idea was widely welcomed, with enormous support from the local farmers, he became chief promoter of the new sugar factory with 10,000 members. i.e. Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Sahkari Sakhar Karkhana Ltd., Arvindnagar, Keshegaon. He also established Dr.BASSK Ltd. Rajrshree Shahu Trust which is an organization registered for social welfare, educational and agricultural R&D as well as promotional activities.

educational and agricultural R&D as well as promotional activities. His dream came into reality and the sugar mill started in 2001. The factory was running successfully with basic motive of cooperative movement with support of modern management concepts, technology and approach.

Today the mill has established itself as a leading cooperative sugar factory not only in Marathwada, but in the entire Maharashtra state, under Mr. Gore's able guidance and vision today it is being recognized for its excellence in technology, management and financial efficiency and highest cane price being paid to cane growing farmers.

Mr. Arvind Gore's contribution is not limited to the activities of the sugar mill alone. He has contributed significantly to a number of social causes, through Rajrshree Shahu Trust to improve the socio-economic status and life standard of the rural population. He is working for rural youths to give them better education. He is also president of Punyashlok Charitable Trust, Gorewadi and Secretary of Terna Public Charitable Trust, Ternanagar. Of Tq/Dist. Osmanabad with sincere involvement in establishment and to functioning of varies kinds of educational institutes and facilities through it. The Terna Charitable Trust has Medical, Engineering, Dental, Arts, Sciences, Commerce Collages and allied post graduation educational institutions at Osmanabad and New-Mumbai of Maharashtra state.

He is associated with the number of organizations involved in activities like social work, sports promotions and cultural activities with active participation in political field. He is participated in varies rallies, marches, Andholan to the Burning Problems of society and farmers. In the year 1990 there was a huge problem of extra cane production which has been not crushed through sugar factories in Osmanabad district. There was Andolan and rallies made by cane growers in the leadership of him to solve the extra cane production issue. Due to this Andolan in Maharasha first time in the history state government declared the subsidy to non crushed cane of cane growers and a policy of extra cane production of cane growers.

His work and devotion to enhance life of poorest of poor peoples in drought prone & under developed district of rural Maharashtra was highly appreciated by renowned institutes and he was awarded by many lifetime achievement awards.
