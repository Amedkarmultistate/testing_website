The society is registered under Multi State Co-op. Societies Act 2002 vide registration no MSCS/CR/484/2012 dated 30.01.2012 and started transacting the business in the year 2011-12, by one branch. Initially we have concentrated on osmanabad block the sugar cane grower's belt for easy cane payment facility to members as well as to transporters of Ambedkar Sugar factory. Then it was expanded in all blocks in Osmanabad District and at Latur, Solapur, Pune and Bidar. Society has started 52 branches in Maharashtra and Karnataka state. Society has made available banking facility to the overall peoples in the area of our branches.

We have adopted the latest technology and our all branches providing services such as Core-Banking, Cheque collection, R.T.G.S./ N.E.F.T , Funds Transfer on lowest service charges. We have experienced positive & good response from the customers, depositors. We are also providing services such as ATM, Mega Banking, and Lockers etc.
